Access Control System

Intelligent controllers replaced at both properties with Mercury MP1502 units. Existing subcontrollers retained and flashed to Genetec firmware. All access door and elevator cab card readers upgraded to HID Signo over OSDP protocol for enhanced tamper detection. Cardholder database rebuilt from the ground up.
